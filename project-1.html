Beauty and science

This photoshoot is full of mysterious vibes. It was held  in the small port located next to a World Ocean Museum in the foggy winter evening, when the darkness was about to come.

It's all about combining model's unique elegance with aesthetics of coldness and  industrial ambience with a pinch of hidden geographical elements. There is a metaphor that beauty, science and technology can easily exist all together.
