Mobile App Prototype

UXD ToolBox is an application for Communication & Multimedia Design students which helps to store all the necessary information regarding the theory of design thinking process.

It is connected with an OSIRIS account so the app is personalized. Students can make notes during lectures with this application and manage their data by adding and deleting creative techniques. The UXD ToolBox is simple, minimalistic and easy to use.
